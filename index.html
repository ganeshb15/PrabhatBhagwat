About the Artist

Hi, this is my dad and I call him “Appa’ for the world he is ‘Mr. Prabhat Bhagwat’ a retired deputy General manager with 31 years of service to the Airport Authority India. He is also an artist, a brilliant one. His art varies in flavor,techinque as well as theme. As for the world, he is a silent man, but for his family, he has a lot of opinions, stories, and ideology, as he taught me history or with my bedtime stories, which were existing and very unique. His knowledge and passion for Indian History, Literature, and mythology are endless.

About his art

But the only way you can see this is through his artwork. Each painting has a story to tell, hidden deep in books. Every piece of history revived as it should be. At a glance, you would love to know more about historical stories like ‘Curzon Wylie ‘.Or you can be amazed by the details of the the’Marikaba Painting’. Or the tales sung by Kannada poet D.R.Bendre. Each painting has a tail to tell, please be eager for each new one coming up. My dad’s only way to talk to the world is through art. Please talk back if you want to. On the right is a painting he has draw of this mentor Late M. T. V. Acharya .
